Peanut Butter and Jelly!!!

Peanut butter and jelly go to together like Bonnie and Clyde or Van Halen and his guitar. A classic combo and if you have ever been confused on how to make a peanut butter and jelly sandwich well then you're in luck! In this here recipe guide you will learn to make the best PB&J you have ever had.

While it may seem simple, this classic dish is a surefire crowdpleaser (assuming you don't have anyone with nut allergies). The peanut butter and jelly is also a versatile vessel for many of your other favorite foods. I recommend trying some wild flavors once you feel comfortable with the base recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of your favorite bread
  • Jar of Peanut Butter
  • Jar of Jelly
  • Not really an ingredient but a knife is necessary. I recommend a butter knife but you may use any standard kitchen knife.

Steps

  1. Lay out your bread side by side.
  2. Open Peanut Butter Jar
  3. Carefully take a knife full of peanut butter and spread onto the left piece of bread. Then repeat with a small amount of peanut butter on the remaining piece of bread.
  4. Wash the knife.
  5. Open jelly jar.
  6. Now carefully scoop jelly out of jar with knife and lay jelly on top of the peanut butter.
  7. When happy with jelly amount, close both jars and put them away.
  8. Then connect bread pieces peanut butter to peanut butter side so that all fillings are on the inside of the sandwich.
  9. Cut sandwich in half diagonally
  10. Eat and enjoy!
