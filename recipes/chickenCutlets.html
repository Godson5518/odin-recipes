Chicken Cutlets

Chicken cutlets are one of life's little pleasures. And when they are done well they can be otherworldly. Chicken cutlets remind me of my childhood. Growing up my mother used to make them on every Sunday, which for an italian women was a little strange. She was never the one to make Sunday sauce, but she always had a plate of fried chicken cutlets ready for her 3 boys.

A chicken cutlet is a blank slate on which to paint with flavors. It can be elevated to a level beyond imagination with any amount of skill (or spice). There are many recipes that call for a chicken cutlet as the base. This recipe will be the best baseline to make those dishes. Or to just enjoy as their own meal!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Eggs
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Olive Oil
  • (optional)Spices of your choice

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl. Whisk until fully yellow.
  2. Pour breadcrumbs into a seperate bowl. Add spices to breadcrumbs if you have them.
  3. Place pan on medium heat and wait for it to be slightly hot.
  4. Pour olive oil into pan.
  5. Take a chicken breast and first put it into the eggs. Then cover with breadcrumbs.
  6. Put that piece of chicken into the pan and cook until golden brown.
  7. Repeat steps 5 and 6 until all chicken has been cooked. You can add eggs and breadcrumbs to your bowl as needed.
  8. Serve and Enjoy!
